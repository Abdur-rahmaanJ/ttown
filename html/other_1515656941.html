Disastrous Disasters BETA
Today I present you a plugin which I made aiming to have more interesting gameplay in TheoTown. Disastrous Disasters! This plugin adds random disasters, and even better, you can customize it! After you place the building found in disasters category, you can select the chance of disasters to spawn (from 0.25x to HELL), also you can choose which disasters should spawn and some other options (currently only Russian Roulette). I hope you like it! Please comment your thoughts about the customizable settings which I added, because I really like it. Tested by: DSA Disaster Engineers and KoalaPlugins team. How to install in your city: 1. After installing the plugin, place the building from disasters category somewhere in your city, I recommend the end of the map. 2. Unpause the game and wait until you get a notification "Disastrous Disasters plugin has been installed!" 3. Adjust the settings how you like. The yellow light tile shows current state ( yellow - settings not set up, red - missing tiles, green - working), the "0x" shows current chance for disasters to spawn,tap on it to adjust the chances, tap on the settings tile to adjust which disasters can spawn and tap on tile with red X to reset settings. Have fun! Please remember that this plugin is still under beta testing!!! Download BETA here
