Rural Housing Pack
Hi, I have a present for you all this December. I present to you, folks, Rural Housing. Now you can build homes for the villagers in rural areas. Rural homes can house from one to five villagers depending on the type. There are housings that can only house one villager, guess he is a loner and some housings can house two, four or five villagers. Advantages of rural housing: 1. Does not need roads. Hence you can place them on any ground types you want without a road connection similar to rural villages in real life. 2. No electricity needed. Most of the rural villages are used to a traditional lifestyle and would want to keep it their it their way. 3.Build is auto false. The rural housing will not build itself as to not impede the building of other level 1 houses. This means that you have more control in placing your rural houses. Disadvantages: 1.Low population density. Rural villages are unsuitable if you want a booming population. If you want to control the population you can use rural housing to do it. 2. Some rural houses still needs water, except for leantos and woodhuts. Means you need a clean water supply for those houses. Types of rural housing 1.Leantos- Colorful roof, fast made shelter but can only house a lonely guy. Lonely people works as lumberjack or hunter in the rural community. 2.Woodhuts- Rural house made of planks and wood, can only house two people 3.Shanty house- Big brother of woodhut. 4.Bomb ruins- A remnant of some old war, some villagers still choose to live inside it. 5.Skumole-A place for reformed criminals, inspiration from Skumole Shack in GTA VC. 6.Stonehut- A medieval stone hut, probably houses a wizard family or a group of farmers. 7.SHC hovels- Replica of hovels from Stronghold Crusader that will house some peasants. 8.Wood resind- Two villagers and three workers. This is just a test to see if workers can fit in a residential type. This rural housing pack also includes pedestrian spawners. Pedestrian spawners use the woodhut and hovel texture. Also, I want to give credit to @KINGTUT10101 for the json code on pedestrians, since I used his code to experiment with my own residential pedestrian spawner. Download rural housing pack here: ruralhousing.zip (57.39 KiB) Downloaded 109 times Again, feel free to try my plugin. I hope you guys have fun with it. Also Merry Christmas and Happy Holidays to all Theonians.
