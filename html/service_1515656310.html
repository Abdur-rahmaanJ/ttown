mdk_813
St. Theo General Hospital mdk_813
Hi, as I am not really a fan of the current large hospital's design (no offense, it just looks a bit outdated), I decided to make a new large hospital. It roughly has a 30% larger radius and an approx. 20% larger capacity than the ingame hospital. But, it also costs a bit more and it requires a city rank of 7. So, nothing good comes free, right? I wasn't able to determine whether the blue or the grey version was better, so I decided to offer both for download. You can choose your favorite or you can use both at the same time, which I would probably recommend as long as you have enough plugin texture to spare, because it would increase building variety. The plugins also include trees and an emergency vehicle by Lobby and Theo. PLEASE NOTE: Both versions require the MyRedLight animation, which you need to download separately from here: viewtopic.php?f=73&t=4476 You cannot use the hospitals without this animation, because otherwise it would produce an error and the game won't start!!! Have fun!
