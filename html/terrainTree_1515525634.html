Parking Lots UPDATE [Terrain] by mdk_813
Hi there, today I have a terrain-tool that might be useful for anyone who was ever unnerved by placing a larger number of deco parking lots before. YES! Your prayers have been answered! Now you can finally bring the progress of civilization into your cities and conveniently turn all the ugly and dirty landscape into neatly arranged parking lots. Parking lots as far as the eye can see... The tool works just like the trees from the terrain menu. You can draw out zones that are filled randomly with 1x1 parking lots or you can pick specific variants to build. The lots do not have any influence on anything. They are pure eye-candy. In case the first build was not varied enough you can just zone over the parts that are bothering you until you reach a satisfying result. The graphics were for the most part originally made by Lobby and Theo. I just had the idea and compiled the various lots to form this tool. Most lots are (more or less) the same as the existing deco parking lots, but I added some new ones for the sake of variety. In total, this tool contains 17 different 1x1 lots (from A to Q). One blank lot and 16 lots that are filled with one or two cars. Have fun! UPDATE This is a very important update, I highly recommend redownloading to anyone who has downloaded the plugin before! The update fixes the "auto build" option in order to prevent the parking lots from spawning when creating a new city with trees. Sorry, for the inconvenience!

I would like to give a special thanks to CommanderABab, without whom I would not have been able to complete this plugin! Have fun! P.S.: The rocks in the picture below are no part of the plugin.
